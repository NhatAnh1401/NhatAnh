FAQ

Common questions

Does Anthropic use my API data for training?+
No. Enterprise API traffic is never used to train or fine-tune Claude models. This commitment is contractually guaranteed in the Enterprise tier and documented in our data processing addendum (DPA).
How does Claude compare to GPT-4 or Gemini?+
Claude leads on context length (200K tokens), long-document reasoning, and constitutional alignment. The right choice depends on your workload — we encourage benchmarking on your production tasks before committing.
Can I deploy Claude in my own VPC or on-prem?+
Yes. Enterprise customers can deploy Claude in dedicated cloud environments or on-premises via our AWS and GCP Marketplace listings and private deployment programs. Contact sales for architecture details.
What are the rate limits on Professional?+
Professional tier includes priority rate limits — typically 10× higher than Starter, with burst capacity. Exact limits depend on model selection. Custom throughput lanes are available on Enterprise.
How do I migrate from another LLM provider?+
Claude's API structure is similar to OpenAI's, making migration straightforward for most teams. We provide a migration guide, a compatibility layer, and dedicated onboarding support on Professional and Enterprise tiers.
Is EU data residency available?+
Yes. EU data residency is available on the Enterprise tier with processing in AWS eu-west-1 (Ireland) or eu-central-1 (Frankfurt). A GDPR-compliant DPA is provided as standard.
Can I fine-tune Claude on proprietary data?+
Fine-tuning is available in private beta for qualified Enterprise customers. System prompts, few-shot examples, and RAG address most customization needs effectively without training overhead.
